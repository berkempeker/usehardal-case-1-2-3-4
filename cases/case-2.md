## CASE 2: Traffic Booster

### Scenario:

Hardal has most of its traffic from Turkey. We want to increase our traffic from Europe & United States and create awareness and inbound traffic.

### Challenge:

Design a go-to-market growth strategy to acquire 30 paying EU/US customers within 90 days.

### Your Task:

- Design the strategy: how you find prospects, generate leads, and convert them
- Define what success looks like at 30 / 60 / 90 days

### Deliverables:

- Channel strategy with rationale (why these channels over others at this stage)
- Execution plan with concrete tactics and owner actions
- Metrics framework: what do you measure to know it's working before conversions happen?
