## CASE 3: $100K Monthly Paid Marketing Budget Allocation

### Scenario:

Hardal has allocated $100,000/month for paid marketing to increase qualified leads. Current metrics:

- Website traffic: 50,000/month
- Demo  requests: 200/month (0.4% conversion)
- Qualified leads: 80/month (40% of demos)
- Average deal size: $15,000/year
- Sales cycle: 60 days

### Challenge:

Allocate the $100K budget across channels to maximize qualified leads.

### Your Task:

- Budget allocation by channel (LinkedIn, Google Ads, Linkedin Influencers, Reddit etc.)
- Reasoning for each allocation
- Expected ROI and lead projections
- Month 1 vs Month 3 vs Month 6 strategy
- Testing budget vs scaling budget split
- Success metrics and KPIs

### Deliverables:

- Budget allocation spreadsheet with channel strategy and expected metrics
- 90-day execution roadmap with risk analysis
